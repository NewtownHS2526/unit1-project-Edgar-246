About Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi was known for freeing his country India from British rule in the 20th century through acts of non-violents. He was sent to prison many times because of his protests but his teachings inspired many people and other activists later on such as Martin Luther King Jr. which would shape our world for how it is today.

  • Founded the spritial monastery to all people.
